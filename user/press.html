Welcome to the Newsroom where users, journalists, developers, and other interested parties can review the latest information and news from the Piccolo community. 

If you would like to receive our news releases by email - we can add you to our email distribution list. Sign up on the Piccolo Announcement mailing list. This list is used infrequently, less than once a month, for key announcements about new releases, new applications, etc.
